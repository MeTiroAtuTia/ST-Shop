[size=13pt][color=red]Introduction[/color][/size]
   [size=8pt]ST Shop allows you to add a shop to your forum and gives you a wide variety of options to customize your shop for your users experience.[/size]
[/size]
[hr]
[size=13pt][color=red]Features[/color][/size]
  [size=11pt]- Enable Shop[/size]
      [size=10pt]- You can use the shop features as a standalone points system[/size]
  [size=11pt]- Custom credits/currency[/size]
  [size=11pt]- Add/Edit shop items[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Image[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Price[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Categories[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Stock[/size]
  [size=11pt]- Purchase items from the shop[/size]
  [size=11pt]- Custom categories[/size]
  [size=11pt]- Trade center[/size]
  [size=11pt]- Shop logs[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Transaction logs[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Bank logs[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Trade logs[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Gift logs[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Sent credits logs[/size]
  [size=11pt]- Casino/games room[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Special game pass access to play[/size]
  [size=11pt]- Inventories[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Display inventories in your profile post[/size]
      [size=10pt]- Select your favorite items to display[/size]
  [size=11pt]- Shop stats[/size]
